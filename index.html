FAQ

Fequently Asked Question

We compiled a list of answers to address your most pressing question regarding our Services

Is there a discount for students or groups?

Yes! We offer a 50% discount for verified undergraduate and graduate students. For teams booking 5 or more seats, please check our Team tier or reach out to our support for custom group rates.

Can I switch my ticket tier later?

Yes, you can upgrade your ticket (e.g., from Standard to Pro) at any time before the event starts, as long as seats are available. You will only need to pay the difference in the ticket price.

Will I receive a certificate of attendance?

Yes, all attendees will receive an official DevConf 2026 digital certificate via email. You can easily share it on LinkedIn or include it in your professional portfolio.

Will the session recordings be available after the event?

Absolutely. All Standard, Pro, and Team ticket holders get access to the high-quality digital recordings of all talks and workshops. Recordings will be uploaded to your dashboard within 24 hours after the conference concludes.
